Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

D3V Prints takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order through our website, we collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your order details including product type, color preferences, and any notes
  • Files you upload such as photos or STL files for custom orders
  • Your shipping address, collected separately via email when confirming your order

We do not collect payment information directly through our website. Payment is handled separately via instructions sent to you by email.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to fulfill your order and communicate with you about it. Specifically we use it to:

  • Confirm and process your order
  • Send you updates about your order status
  • Contact you if we have questions about your order
  • Ship your completed order to you

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. File Uploads

Photos and files you upload are processed and stored securely through Uploadcare, a third party file hosting service. Your files are used exclusively for printing your order. We do not share your uploaded files with anyone outside of the order fulfillment process. Files may be retained for a short period after order completion and then deleted.

4. Third Party Services

We use a small number of third party services to run our website and process orders:

  • Web3Forms for processing order form submissions and sending email notifications
  • Uploadcare for secure file storage and delivery
  • Google Sheets for internal order tracking
  • GitHub Pages for website hosting

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

5. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not run ads on our website.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS and your data is handled by reputable third party services. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request that we delete your personal information at any time. To do so, contact us at d3vprints@gmail.com and we will remove your data from our records within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, reach out to us at d3vprints@gmail.com.
